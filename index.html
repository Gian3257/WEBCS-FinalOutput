About Me

Game development started as a hobby, but it quickly became one of my greatest passions.
I began learning game development on my own during 2020 by watching countless YouTube
tutorials, experimenting and making various prototype games with the Unity game engine.
I’ve found joy in every step of the process and I’m constantly exploring new ideas and bringing
them to life through indie game development.

I primarily work with Unity, which enables me to create interactive and engaging games.
I also use Blender for 3D modeling and animation, which allows me to create environments,
and assets.

Although I approach game development as a hobby, my projects are built with technical detail.
I try to make each game an enjoyable and exciting experience.

My Projects

Mobile Endless Runner

An endless runner mobile game. Platforms and obstacles are randomly generated. Avoid and shoot obstacles with your gun
and try to survive as long as you can. The object pooling pattern is implemented to offer performance boost and optimization.
Sound effects were also made using the SFXR software.

Multiplayer PvP

A multiplayer sword fight prototype game where you’re able to swing your sword to damage other players and block other
players’ attacks. Multiplayer is implemented using the Photon Engine, a multiplayer service provider.

FPS Parkour

A 3D Parkour FPS game where you’re able to slide down obstacles, run along walls, and swing or grapple along buildings and
obstacles with your grappling hand. The grappling hand model and animations were made in Blender.
